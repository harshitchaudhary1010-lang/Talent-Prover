PROBLEM:
After logging in as a student, you were not being redirected to the student
dashboard page, or you were getting a 404 error.

ROOT CAUSE:
Case sensitivity mismatch between URLs and folder names.
- The folder name is: "Dashboard" (capital D)
- But URLs were using: "/dashboard/" (lowercase d)

On Windows, file paths are case-insensitive, but URLs in web browsers ARE
case-sensitive. So /dashboard/student.php would not work even though
Dashboard\student.php exists on your computer.
